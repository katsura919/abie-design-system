02.2 ~ Typography

Three families.
Two weights. Done.

Restraint is the point. With this much constraint, the type itself becomes the brand mark.

~ Sans · UI & headlines

Host Grotesk

font-black UPPERCASE, tracking-tighter. Used for every headline, button label, nav.

Weights: 400, 900 only. No 500. No 600.
~ Serif · Accent & body

Instrument Serif

italic, lowercase. Lives inside headlines as accent words; runs body when narrative.

Italic only. Body bumps +6% size for legibility.
~ Mono · Labels

Geist Mono

9–11px, UPPERCASE, tracking-widest. Section numbers, captions, slide counters.

Never below 9px. Never as body copy.
